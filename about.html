About Me

Hello! My name is Coda, and I’m a 20-year-old student currently pursuing a degree in Web Development at University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point. I specialize in front-end design and development, with a strong interest in creating user-centered, accessible, and visually engaging digital experiences.

My skills

I am primarily a front-end developer who works with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Some things I have done include:

  • User Research & Testing
    Personas | Journey Mapping | Competitive Analysis | Usability Testing
  • Design & Prototyping
    Wireframing | Low/High-Fidelity Prototypes | Figma
  • Front-End Development
    HTML | CSS | JavaScript
I have also worked as a back-end developer on an agile team, using C# and MySQL to build and maintain databases.

A Little Extra
